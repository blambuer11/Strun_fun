Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start earning with Strun?
  1. Create an account and connect your Solana wallet
  2. Enable location permissions
  3. Start a run to claim land parcels
  4. Complete AI-generated tasks at specific locations
  5. Earn SOL, USDC, and XP rewards
What is x402 payment?

x402 is our instant on-chain payment system. When accessing tasks on privately owned land parcels, you pay rent in USDC directly to the land owner. Payments are verified on the Solana blockchain in seconds.

How do I mint a Land NFT?

Land NFTs are automatically generated when you run in an area. The app tracks your route using H3 hexagonal grid system. Each parcel you traverse can be claimed as an NFT, giving you ownership and the right to earn rent from future users.

Why isn't my wallet connecting?

Solutions:

  • Ensure you have Phantom or Solflare wallet app installed
  • Check that you're on Solana Devnet (for testing) or Mainnet
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting
  • Restart the app
My task verification failed. Why?

Common reasons:

  • Location mismatch: You're too far from the task location
  • Photo doesn't match: AI couldn't verify the task requirement
  • GPS spoofing detected: Using fake location tools is prohibited
  • Nonce expired: Request a fresh task claim
How do I withdraw my earnings?

Your SOL and USDC rewards are stored in your connected wallet. You can:

  • View balance in the Dashboard → Wallet section
  • Transfer to another wallet or exchange using your wallet app
  • Use rewards to pay rent for tasks on owned parcels
Is my location data private?

Yes. Location data is only used for:

  • Tracking runs (stored temporarily)
  • Verifying task completion
  • Calculating land ownership

Location history is deleted after 90 days. Only NFT-linked routes are permanent (on blockchain). See our Privacy Policy.

Can I lose my Land NFT?

No. Once minted, Land NFTs are permanently yours (stored in your wallet). However, if you don't set rent or engage with the parcel, you won't earn passive income from it.

What are XP and Levels?

XP (experience points) are earned by completing tasks and runs. Every 100 XP = 1 level. Higher levels unlock:

  • Exclusive badges
  • Better rewards
  • Special community features
  • Priority task access
How do I report a bug or issue?

Email us at support@strun.app with:

  • Device model and OS version
  • App version
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots (if applicable)

Troubleshooting

App won't open or crashes

  1. Update to the latest version
  2. Clear app cache
  3. Restart your device
  4. Reinstall the app (your wallet data is safe)

GPS tracking inaccurate

  1. Enable High Accuracy mode in device settings
  2. Check that location permissions are granted
  3. Ensure GPS signal is strong (outdoor, clear sky)
  4. Disable battery saving mode

Payment not confirmed

  1. Check transaction status on Solana Explorer
  2. Verify you used the correct memo format
  3. Wait for blockchain confirmation (usually <1 min)
  4. Contact support if stuck after 5 minutes

Account & Refunds

Delete Account

Email support@strun.app from your registered email with "Delete Account" in subject. Note: Blockchain data (transactions, NFTs) cannot be deleted.

Refund Policy

x402 payments are instant and irreversible (blockchain). Refunds are only issued for:

  • Proven technical errors on our end
  • Duplicate payments
  • Payment without access granted

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours with transaction hash.
