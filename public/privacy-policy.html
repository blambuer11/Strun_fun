Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Strun ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect your GPS location data to enable core features:

  • Track running routes and distance
  • Verify task completion at specific locations
  • Mint land NFTs based on areas you've run
  • Calculate parcel ownership

You have full control: Location tracking only occurs when you actively use the app for running or task completion. You can disable location permissions at any time in your device settings.

2.2 Wallet Information

We collect your Solana wallet public key to:

  • Process on-chain transactions (SOL, USDC)
  • Mint NFTs to your wallet
  • Track your rewards and payments

Security: We never store your private keys. All transactions require your explicit approval via your wallet app.

2.3 User-Generated Content

  • Profile information (username, bio, avatar)
  • Photos/proofs uploaded for task verification
  • Community posts and comments

2.4 Technical Data

  • Device type, OS version
  • App version and crash reports
  • IP address (for fraud prevention)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide Services: Enable running tracking, task verification, and rewards
  • Blockchain Transactions: Process SOL/USDC payments and NFT minting
  • Anti-Fraud: Detect GPS spoofing, fake photos, and suspicious activity
  • Support: Respond to your inquiries and resolve issues
  • Improvements: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app features

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Blockchain: Public transaction data (wallet addresses, amounts) on Solana
  • Service Providers: AWS, Supabase (database), Pinata (IPFS storage)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

5. Blockchain & Public Data

Important: Blockchain transactions are public and permanent. This includes:

  • Your wallet address
  • Transaction amounts and timestamps
  • NFT metadata (land parcels, run records)

We recommend using a separate wallet for privacy if concerned about public transaction history.

6. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Transaction Records: Permanent (on blockchain)
  • Location History: Deleted after 90 days unless associated with NFT
  • Verification Photos: Retained for 30 days, then deleted

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (Note: blockchain data cannot be deleted)
  • Withdraw Consent: Disable location permissions or disconnect wallet

Contact us at privacy@strun.app to exercise these rights.

8. Children's Privacy

Strun is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure backend infrastructure
  • Regular security audits

However: No system is 100% secure. Protect your wallet private keys and never share them.

10. International Users

Strun operates globally. Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. By using the app, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via in-app message. Continued use after changes means acceptance.

12. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with:

  • Solana Blockchain: Public ledger (see Solana privacy policy)
  • Wallet Providers: Phantom, Solflare (see their privacy policies)
  • Maps: Google Maps API (see Google's privacy policy)

13. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Analytics (anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

14. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Contact us:

  • Email: privacy@strun.app
  • Support: https://strun.app/support

15. Compliance

We comply with:

  • GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • KVKK (Turkey Personal Data Protection Law)
